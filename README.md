# StudyCircle

> A collaborative learning platform that helps university students connect, share ideas, organize study sessions, and learn together.

---

# Overview

StudyCircle is a student-focused platform designed to improve collaboration and academic engagement within universities.

The platform creates a digital space where students can:

- Create and join study sessions
- Connect with classmates and study partners
- Share academic ideas and resources
- Build collaborative learning communities
- Improve productivity through group learning

StudyCircle aims to make learning more interactive, social, and accessible.

---

# Problem Statement

Many university students struggle with:

- Studying alone without support
- Difficulty finding study partners
- Poor communication between classmates
- Lack of organized collaborative learning tools
- Limited engagement outside classrooms

Most existing platforms are either too general or not built specifically for academic collaboration.

StudyCircle solves this by providing a dedicated environment focused on student interaction and shared learning.

---

# Solution

StudyCircle provides a centralized platform where students can:

- Discover and join study groups
- Organize learning sessions
- Share educational content and ideas
- Communicate with peers in real time
- Build academic communities around courses and interests

The goal is to encourage collaborative learning and improve the overall university learning experience.

3. Students connect and collaborate
